01

Wine Art

True two-in-one, the Wine Art is equipped with an exclusive system of ideal serving temperatures with 2 independent compartments allowing you to program the temperature adapted to each wine (white or red). This small wine bar with its soft and timeless design allows you to keep two opened bottles for up to 10 days* The effectiveness of the Wine Art has been scientifically proven by the Cellule d'Expertise Scientifique en Œnologie (CESEO) of the Institut Universitaire de la Vigne et du Vin (Dijon, France).

02

Pure Range

Dedicated to the aging and serving of wine, the Pure range recreates the exact conditions of a natural cellar. In addition, it saves up to 55% of energy compared to a traditional electric cellar.
Thanks to technological innovations such as a slow cycle compressor, a thicker structure with better insulation and the use of R600a gas, the most environmentally friendly and least harmful gas for the atmosphere. The Pure range has one of the lowest noise levels and a look as refined as it is contemporary.

03

Premiere Range

In creating this range, we wanted to offer a chic and customizable cellar with a wide choice of modern and design finishes, but also a low energy consumption cellar contributing to the protection of the environment.
Available with solid or glass doors, in small, medium or large capacity, the Première range fits into all types of interiors. It respects the 5 essential criteria for the proper aging of your wines, recreating a protective atmosphere. Beneath its graphic and colourful lines lies all of EuroCave's expertise.

FEATURES
WHY USE EUROCAVE?
EuroCave wine cabinets are a top choice of true connoisseurs. Have confidence when choosing this quality French-made brand. Every detail of proper wine storage was considered and achieved in the making of these cabinets

Consistent Temperature

Keep the temperature consistent, even when temperatures outside the cabinet range from 0° – 35°C! Eurocave ensures this with both heating and cooling processes and SRA aluminum walls for even temperature distribution.

Thermal Insulation, UV Protection

Eurocave’s CQI cellular insulant plus the 5cm cabinet walls are equivalent to almost 2 metres of solid earth!

Permanent ventilation system

Eurocave cabinets come with a separate vent for air intake and air output. The intake vent also includes a charcoal filter for purifying the air.

Suitable humidity levels

Achieve a relative humidity consistently over 50% with stucco aluminum walls for even moisture distribution.

Anti-vibration system

Let your wine rest in peace. The compressor is completely separated from the cabinet and stands on high performance silent blocks.

Alarm System

Left the door ajar? Humidity level gone wrong? Too warm or cold in the cabinet? An alarm is triggered to let you know right away (with exception of Premiere line).
